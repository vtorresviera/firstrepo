Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

The best option at any time :)

Ingredients

Quantity Ingredient
2 slices bread
2 tbs peanut butter
2 tbs fruit jelly

Instructions

  1. Spread the peanut butter in one of the slices.
  2. Spread the fruit jelly in the other slice.
  3. Put the slices together.
